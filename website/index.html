Audio comparisons made beautiful

Compare up to 32 tracks

Instant A/B switching during playback lets you hear differences in mix and sound quality. Takes supports local files and streaming music from Apple Music, Spotify, and YouTube.

Selection looping

Loop the exact section you care about so small differences in arrangement, mix, or mastering are easy to hear.

Blind listening test mode

Randomize order and hide track details to listen without bias. Reveal track names after you pick a take.

Auto-align tracks

Automatically line up waveforms with a single click. Smart analysis takes care of the work.

Beautiful native UI

Designed for macOS with a polished native interface, and full light and dark mode support.

Rapid navigation

Keep your workflow moving with quick open from the Finder and Apple Music, full keyboard shortcuts, and drag and drop support.
